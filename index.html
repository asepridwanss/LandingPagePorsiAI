How It Works

  1. 1

    Login

    Sign in to the platform using your student account to access the thesis formatting assistant. Only registered users from FTI UNIBBA can access the system to ensure data security and proper usage.

  2. 2

    Paste Your Thesis Text

    Copy and paste the part of your thesis that you want to format.

  3. 3

    Choose the Section

    Select which part of the thesis the text belongs to (e.g., Abstract, Chapter 1: Background, Problem Formulation, Literature Review, etc.). This helps the AI know the correct formatting structure based on the official guide.

  4. 4

    Choose AI Model

    Select the AI engine that will process your text. You can choose between:

    • ChatGPT – reliable and widely used.
    • Gemini – smart and responsive with context handling.
    • DeepSeek – optimized for structured and technical texts.

    Each model offers different strengths. Try them out and compare results!

  5. 5

    Get the Result Instantly

    Sistem ini akan secara instan mengembalikan teks yang telah diformat ulang berdasarkan pedoman penulisan tesis FTIF/UNIBBA. Anda dapat menyalin hasilnya. Hemat waktu, kurangi revisi, dan tingkatkan kualitas dengan hanya satu klik!
